Est. 2020 • Philippines

Rooted in Goodness.

EarthHaven began with a simple question: "Why is it so hard to find vegetables that actually taste like vegetables?"

We started as a small cooperative of three families. Today, we define the standard for clean, ethical, and delicious produce.

Our Mission

To democratize access to organic food by building a direct supply chain that empowers local farmers and nourishes urban families.

Our Vision

A future where clean, chemical-free food is the standard, not the luxury, for every Filipino household.
